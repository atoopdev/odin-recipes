Pumpkin Bread

Description

It is not fall without a delicious pumpkin bread recipe. This recipe has the best pumpkin flavor in my opinion. I have made this recipe twice in the past month or so. In my experience this loaf lasts very well in the refrigerator - if you don't eat it all at once that is.

I recommend slightly reducing the final sugar/cinnamon topping as I found a lot of it falls off right away after baking - but its up to you!

Ingredients

  • 15oz can pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup neutral cooking oil or melted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 2/3c sugar
  • 1 1/2tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp (heaped) nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp (heaped) ground ginger
  • 2 pinches ground cloves
  • 2 1/4c all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Oil or butter a 6 cup loaf pan.
  2. Combine pumpkin, oil, eggs, and sugar in a mixing bowl. Add remaining dry ingredients together in a separate bowl, gently mix. Slowly sprinkle dry ingredients into wet, mixing and pouring in turn, until everything is mixed.
  3. Scrape batter into loaf pan. Mix 1 Tbsp cinnamon and 1 Tbsp sugar together in a small bowl. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar mix on top of batter.
  4. Bake for 65 to 75 minutes. Cook until knife poked into the middle comes out clean.
  5. Allow to cool for 10min before removing from pan - but loaf cake is just fine cooling in the pan.
  6. Enjoy!
